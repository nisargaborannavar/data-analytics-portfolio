Analysis¶

In [5]:
# Chart 1 - Sales by Category
df.groupby('Category')['Sales'].sum().plot(kind='bar', color='steelblue')
plt.title('Total Sales by Category')
plt.xlabel('Category')
plt.ylabel('Sales')
plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()

Insights¶

  • Technology has the highest total sales among all categories
  • The West region generates the most profit
  • Sales and Profit have a weak positive relationship — high sales does not always mean high profit
