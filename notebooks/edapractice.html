Insights¶

- Copiers has the highest profit among the Sub - Catgeory¶
- West Region has the highest sales compared to other regions¶
- The more discount given, the more likely the order loses money. Orders with 0 discount are the most profitable. Discounts above 50% almost always result in a loss.¶
